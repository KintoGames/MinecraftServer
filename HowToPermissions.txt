TO do permissions in this verions of github.

The main permission is globalsgroups.yml.

example:
groups:

  g:essentials_default:
    permissions:
    - essentials.help
    - essentials.helpop
    - essentials.list
    - essentials.motd
    - essentials.rules
    - essentials.spawn
    - groupmanager.notify.self
    - heroes.classes.*
    - heroes.bind
